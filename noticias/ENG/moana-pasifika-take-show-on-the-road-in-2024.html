Moana Pasifika take show on the road in 2024

6 December 2023 · ALLBLACKS.COM
Moana Pasifika will connect with more fans than ever when taking their home Super Rugby Pacific games to different venues next year.

Now preparing for their third DHL Super Rugby Pacific season, their first under new coach and former All Blacks captain Tana Umaga, Moana Pasifika, will base their high-performance activity at North Harbour Stadium in Albany.

They will play their first home game in Albany against the Rebels on March 8.

They will also play their home against the Blues at Eden Park on March 30, during Easter, with a 4.35 pm start.

On April 12, they will play the Reds at Semenoff Stadium in Whangārei.

Their home game on May 4 against the Highlanders will be played at a yet-to-be-named offshore venue.

Moana Pasifika open their campaign against the Highlanders in Dunedin on February 24.
